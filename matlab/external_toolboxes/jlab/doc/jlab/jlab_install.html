JLAB_INSTALL  Instructions for installing JLAB.

   In your 'startup.m' file, you should put the lines:
 
          addpath /your/path/to/jlab
          jlab_addpath

   The second line adds the various JLAB subdirectories to your search path. 
  
   Then please type 'jlab_runtests tests' at the Matlab command prompt and
   copy and paste the output to me at eponym@jmilly.net.
