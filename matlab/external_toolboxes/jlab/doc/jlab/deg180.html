deg180 

DEG180  Converts degrees to the range [-180,180].

   [TH1,TH2,...THN]=DEG180(TH1,TH2,...THN) converts the input
   angles, which are measured in degrees, to the range [-180, 180].

   See also DEG360.

   'deg180 --t' runs a test.
