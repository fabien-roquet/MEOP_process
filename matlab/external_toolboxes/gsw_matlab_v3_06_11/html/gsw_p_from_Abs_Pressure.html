USAGE:

p = gsw_p_from_Abs_Pressure(Absolute_Pressure)

DESCRIPTION:

Calculates sea pressure from Absolute Pressure.  Note that Absolute 
Pressure is in Pa NOT dbar.

INPUT:

Abs_Pressure  =  Absolute Pressure                                      [ Pa ]

OUTPUT:

p  =  sea pressure                                                    [ dbar ]

EXAMPLE:

p = [100000; 500000; 1250000; 2500000; 6000000; 1000000;]
p = gsw_p_from_Abs_Pressure(Absolute_Pressure)
p =
   10
   50
  125
  250
  600
 1000
