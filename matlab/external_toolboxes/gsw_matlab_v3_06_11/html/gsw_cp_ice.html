USAGE:

cp_ice = gsw_cp_ice(t,p)

DESCRIPTION:

Calculates the isobaric heat capacity of ice.

INPUT:

t    =   in-situ temperature (ITS-90)                          [ deg C ]
p    =   sea pressure                                           [ dbar ]
        ( ie. absolute pressure - 10.1325 dbar )
p may have dimensions 1x1 or Mx1 or 1xN or MxN, where t is MxN.

OUTPUT:

cp_ice   =   heat capacity of ice                           [ J/(kg*K) ]

EXAMPLE:

t = [-10.7856; -13.4329; -12.8103; -12.2600;  -10.8863;  -8.4036;]
p = [      10;       50;      125;      250;       600;     1000;]
cp_ice = gsw_cp_ice(t,p)
cp_ice =
  1.0e+003 *
   2.017314262094657
   1.997830122682709
   2.002281331375396
   2.006127319545421
   2.015676303959609
   2.033308170371559
